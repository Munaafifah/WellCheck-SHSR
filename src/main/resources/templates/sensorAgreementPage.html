Sensor Data Agreement

Health Sensor Data Collection & Usage Agreement
Please read this agreement carefully before registering your sensor device.
1. Introduction
This agreement governs the collection, use, and storage of health data generated by your registered sensor device within the WellCheck Smart Health Remote System (SHRS). By agreeing, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and consent to the terms outlined below. This agreement is aligned with current health data privacy standards for citizen-generated health data collected through wearable sensor devices (Brückner et al., 2023).
2. Data We Collect
Your registered health sensor device will continuously monitor and transmit the following physiological parameters to the WellCheck system:
  • Heart Rate — beats per minute (bpm), monitored in real-time to detect abnormal cardiac activity.
  • Body Temperature — measured in degrees Celsius (°C) to detect fever or hypothermia.
  • Blood Oxygen Level (SpO₂) — percentage of oxygen saturation in the blood.
  • ECG Reading — electrocardiogram data to monitor electrical activity of the heart.
  • Historical Readings — a timestamped log of all sensor readings stored for trend analysis and medical review.
3. How Your Data is Used
Your collected health data will be used exclusively for the following purposes:
  • Enabling your assigned doctor to remotely monitor your health status in real-time.
  • Supporting disease prediction and early detection through the WellCheck ML diagnostic system.
  • Generating alerts and notifications when abnormal readings are detected.
  • Providing historical health trends for medical consultation and prescription purposes.
As noted by Roque & Felt (2025), patients should be meaningfully informed about how their sensor-generated data is collected and used before providing consent. WellCheck is committed to this principle of transparency.
4. Data Storage & Security
  • All sensor data is stored securely in the WellCheck MongoDB database (Wellcheck2) and is accessible only to authorised personnel including your assigned doctor and system administrators.
  • Your data will not be sold, shared, or transferred to any third-party commercial entities.
  • Access to your health data is role-restricted. Only your assigned doctor, clinic assistant, and system admin can view relevant portions of your data.
  • Data is retained for the duration of your active patient record within the WellCheck system.
5. Your Rights as a Patient
In accordance with health data privacy principles (Brückner et al., 2023; Personalized Consent Flow, NIH 2017), you retain the following rights:
  • Right to Access — You may view your sensor readings at any time through your Patient Dashboard.
  • Right to Withdraw — You may contact the system administrator to deregister your sensor device and stop data collection at any time.
  • Right to Transparency — You will be informed of any significant changes to how your data is collected or used.
  • Right to Accuracy — You may request corrections to inaccurate health records through your assigned doctor.
